Ingredients

  • 3 cups basmati rice
  • 2 TBSP olive oil
  • 1 piece cinnamon stick
  • 2 pods green cardamom
  • 2 whole cloves
  • 1 TBSP cumin seed
  • 2 tsp salt (to taste)
  • 5 cups water
  • 1 small onion thinly diced
  • 2 TBSP finely chopped cilantro (optional)

Instructions

  1. Place rice into a bowl with enough water to cover. set aside to soak or 20 minutes.
  2. Heat the oil in a large pot or sauce pan over medium heat. Add the cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, cloves, and cumin seed.
  3. Cook for about a minute, you want to release the flavors; this will happen in a matter of seconds so be ready with the onions
  4. Add the onion to the pot, Saut√© the onions until a rich golden. the cinamon at this point should have unfolded.
  5. Drain the rice and stir into the pot, cook and stir the rice with the onions until roasted, (this will only take a few minutes)
  6. Add the water and the salt to the pot, stir well, making sure that the rice is not sticking to the bottom and bring to a boil
  7. Cover, and reduce heat, simmer for about 15 minutes or until all the water has been absorbed
  8. Let stand for about 5 minutes, then using a fork carefully remove the Cinnamon stick, the cardamom and the cloves they should be 5 pieces in all Fluff with a fork and add in the cilantro if you wish to do so.
  9. Serve as a side with Chicken Tikka Masala
